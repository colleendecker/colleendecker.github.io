Colleen Decker

When her husband decided to grow a beard, Colleen decided to make him some premium beard oil to help keep his mane soft and manageable. After much research, she learned a lot about the benefits of quality carrier and essential oils, and she put together what her husband considered the best beard oil he had ever tried.

That's where it all started, but that's not where it ended!
